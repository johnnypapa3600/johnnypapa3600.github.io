Platform Features

Users get exposure to a pod one of two ways: buying the pod on the open market (like Uniswap or another AMM) or through wrapping. By wrapping, users put up their collateral in exchange for pod tokens in a slippage free manner.

  • Buy pod tokens directly on the open market (i.e. Uniswap)
  • Wrap pod tokens without slippage for a small fee, useful for arbitraging and opening a position with larger size
  • After acquiring pod tokens, sleep well with your desired exposure, or provide LP to start earning yield
